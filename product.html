Product Description

The Premium Full Motion Wall Mount is engineered for optimal TV positioning and viewing experience. This professional-grade mount combines robust construction with smooth articulation mechanisms, allowing you to achieve the perfect viewing angle from any position in your room.

Featuring a dual-arm design with precision bearings, this mount provides exceptional stability while maintaining smooth operation. The mount extends up to 16 inches from the wall and can be collapsed to just 2.8 inches for a low-profile installation.

Installation Guide

Important Safety Notice

Always ensure proper wall stud mounting. Professional installation recommended for safety.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Locate wall studs using a stud finder
  2. Mark mounting hole positions on wall
  3. Drill pilot holes and install wall plate
  4. Attach mounting plate to TV back
  5. Connect TV plate to wall mount
  6. Test movement and adjust tension
  7. Organize cables using provided clips

Tools Required

  • Drill with bits
  • Level (included)
  • Stud finder
  • Screwdriver set
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil for marking
